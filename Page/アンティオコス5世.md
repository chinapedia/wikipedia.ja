**アンティオコス5世 エウパトル** （、[紀元前172年](../Page/紀元前172年.md "wikilink") – [紀元前161年](../Page/紀元前161年.md "wikilink")）\[1\] は[セレウコス朝](../Page/セレウコス朝.md "wikilink")の君主。[紀元前164年](../Page/紀元前164年.md "wikilink")から[紀元前161年](../Page/紀元前161年.md "wikilink")まで在位した 。

[ローマ人](../Page/ローマ人.md "wikilink")により王に任命され\[2\]、を[摂政](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/摂政 "wikilink")として執政した\[3\]。

## 生涯

### 即位

父王の[アンティオコス4世](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/アンティオコス4世 "wikilink")と母の[ラオディケ4世](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/ラオディケ4世 "wikilink")がペルシャ（[パルティア](../Page/パルティア.md "wikilink")）で急死した。そのため、アンティオコス5世が、僅か9歳で王位を継承した。アンティオコス4世にシリアを任されていたが[摂政](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/摂政 "wikilink")を務めた。

[セレウコス4世](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/セレウコス4世 "wikilink")（先々代の王）の息子で正当な後継者、[デメトリオスは](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/デメトリオス1世 "wikilink")[ローマの人質となっていた](../Page/共和政ローマ.md "wikilink")。[ローマ元老院](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/ローマ元老院 "wikilink")は22歳のディメトリウスより少年のアンティオコスと摂政のリュシアスによって統治されるほうが都合が良く、帰国することを拒否した\[4\]。

### 治世

[Antiochus_v.jpg](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Antiochus_v.jpg "fig:Antiochus_v.jpg") At the outset of the reign of Antiochus V, there was an attempt by the Syrians to quell the [Maccabean Revolt](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maccabean_Revolt "wikilink") in [Judea](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/Judea "wikilink"), but this ended in a weak compromise. After a military victory in the Battle of Beth-Zecharia, and the killing of [Eleazar Avaran](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eleazar_Avaran "wikilink"), a brother of Judas Maccabaeus, [Lysias](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lysias_\(Syrian_chancellor\) "wikilink") was informed that Philip (a confidant of [Antiochus IV Epiphanes](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antiochus_IV_Epiphanes "wikilink"), who had accompanied this previous king to conquer Mesopotamia, and had been entrusted prior to the death of the king with the upbringing of Antiochus V), was returning to the capital with the other half of the [Seleucid army](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seleucid_army "wikilink"). [Lysias](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lysias_\(Syrian_chancellor\) "wikilink") felt threatened, and advised Antiochus V to offer peace to the [Jews](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jews "wikilink"). The Jews accepted; however, in order to ensure they would not rise against them again soon, the Syrian king and regent broke their promise and tore down the walls of Jerusalem before leaving.

Upon reaching their own kingdom, Lysias and Antiochus V found Philip in control of the capital Antiochia, but they defeated him and retook the city and kingdom.\[5\]

### 没落と死

When the Roman senate heard that the Syrian kingdom kept more warships and elephants than allowed by the peace treaty of Apamea made in 188 BC, they sent a Roman embassy to travel along the cities of Syria and attempted to cripple Seleucid military power by sinking the Syrians' warships and [hamstringing](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/hamstringing "wikilink") their elephants. Lysias dared do nothing to oppose the Romans, but his subservience so enraged his Syrian subjects that the Roman envoy Gnaeus Octavius (consul of 165 BC) was assassinated in [Laodicea](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/Latakia "wikilink") (162 BC).\[6\]

At this juncture [Demetrius](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/Demetrius_I_of_Syria "wikilink") escaped from [Rome](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rome "wikilink") and was received in [Syria](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/Syria "wikilink") as the true king. Antiochus Eupator was soon put to death together with his protector [Lysias](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lysias_\(Syrian_chancellor\) "wikilink").
