<li>To make a nomination, just add the article/picture/quote in the nominations lists. If it is a picture, add names of photographer and uploader as credits. Please nominate only pictures with proper copyrights/licenses or a public domain image.</li>
</ul>
<dl>
<dt></dt>
<dd><dl>
<dt></dt>
<dd>* Each person may nominate up to THREE items per section. For example, you cannot add more than three articles to the "鳥ピックアップ" list. (as there are 4 sections, your maximun nominations is 3x4 = 12 items)
</dd>
<dd>* If you have created or contributed extensively to the article/picture yourself, please add "yes" in "self-nomination" cell.
</dd>
</dl>
</dd>
</dl>
<ul>
<li>To vote for an item, just add your name in the <strong>"Votes"</strong> cell of that item</li>
</ul>
<dl>
<dt></dt>
<dd><dl>
<dt></dt>
<dd>* Each person may vote for ONE item per section.
</dd>
<dd>* You may change your vote anytime.
</dd>
<dd>* You are not allowed to vote for your self-nominated article/picture.
</dd>
<dd>* The nomination with the most vote in each section will be presented in the portal at the beginning of the month and it will be removed from the nominations lists.
</dd>
<dd>* If two or more items got the same amount of votes, the item higher on the list will be selected
</dd>
<dd>* The Template:birdportal will be posted in the talk page of the selected article/picture to congratulate and show appreciation to the article/picture's contributors. See instruction how to use the template <a href="https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/Portal_talk:bird" title="wikilink">here</a>
</dd>
<dd>* When the item you voted was selected, you may (and should) vote for a new one. So you are recommended to check this page at the beginning of each month.
</dd>
