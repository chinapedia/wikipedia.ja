**ニューカッスル大学**(ニューカッスルだいがく、、略称：**UoN** )は、[オーストラリア](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/オーストラリア "wikilink")、[ニューサウスウェールズ州](../Page/ニューサウスウェールズ州.md "wikilink")[ニューカッスルの郊外](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/ニューカッスル_\(ニューサウスウェールズ州\) "wikilink")、キャラハンに位置する公立の[大学](../Page/大学.md "wikilink")であり、郊外型大学では、国内最大の規模を持つ。

## 組織

### Faculty of Business and Law

The [Faculty of Business and Law](http://www.newcastle.edu.au/faculty/business-law/) contains the following schools:

  - [School of Law](http://www.newcastle.edu.au/school/law/)
  - [School of Business and Management](http://www.newcastle.edu.au/school/sbm/)
  - [Newcastle Graduate School of Business](http://www.newcastle.edu.au/school/ngsb/)
  - [School of Economics, Politics & Tourism](http://www.newcastle.edu.au/school/ept/)

### Faculty of Education and Arts

The [Faculty of Education and Arts](http://www.newcastle.edu.au/faculty/education-arts/) contains the following schools:

  - [Wollotuka School of Aboriginal Studies](http://www.newcastle.edu.au/school/aboriginal-studies/)
  - [School of Drama, Fine Art & Music](http://www.newcastle.edu.au/school/conservatorium/) (incorporating the Conservatorium)
  - [School of Education](http://www.newcastle.edu.au/school/education/)
  - [School of Humanities and Social Science](http://www.newcastle.edu.au/school/hss/)

### Faculty of Engineering and the Built Environment

The [Faculty of Engineering and the Built Environment](http://www.newcastle.edu.au/faculty/engineering/) contains the following schools:

  - [School of Architecture and the Built Environment](http://www.newcastle.edu.au/school/architecture-built-environ/)
  - [School of Engineering](http://www.newcastle.edu.au/school/engineering/)
  - [School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science](http://www.newcastle.edu.au/school/elec-eng-comp-sci/)

### Faculty of Science and Information Technology

The [Faculty of Science and Information Technology](http://www.newcastle.edu.au/faculty/science-it/) contains the following schools:

  - [School of Applied Sciences](http://www.newcastle.edu.au/school/applied-science/)
  - [School of Psychology](http://www.newcastle.edu.au/school/psychology/)
  - [School of Design, Communication and IT](http://www.newcastle.edu.au/school/design-communication-it/)
  - [School of Environmental and Life Sciences](http://www.newcastle.edu.au/school/environ-life-science/)
  - [School of Mathematical and Physical Sciences](http://www.newcastle.edu.au/school/math-physical-science/)

### Faculty of Health

The [Faculty of Health](http://www.newcastle.edu.au/faculty/health/) is the most comprehensive of its kind in Australia and one of the most comprehensive in the world. It contains the following schools:

  - [School of Biomedical Sciences](http://www.newcastle.edu.au/school/biomedical-science/)
  - [School of Health Sciences](http://www.newcastle.edu.au/school/health-science/)
  - [School of Medicine and Public Health](http://www.newcastle.edu.au/school/medprac-pop/) (the home of the University's world renowned [Bachelor of Medicine](http://www.newcastle.edu.au/school/medprac-pop/undergraduateprograms/programoverview.html) program, one of the leaders in Australian medical education)
  - [School of Nursing and Midwifery](http://www.newcastle.edu.au/school/nursing-midwifery/)
