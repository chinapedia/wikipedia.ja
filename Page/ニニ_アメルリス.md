**ニニ・アメルリス**（英: Nini Amerlise, 本名 **Teniqua Teeyana Amerlise Barrett**; 1993年11月3日-　) は、カナダのファッションモデル、タレント、起業家。

ロザリー・バレットとケビン・パーチメントとの間に生まれた。アメルリスは２０１７年３月の*スーパー・モデル・カナダ*\[1\] のシーズン１の後にデビューする。2016年の書籍 *The Designer's Orginal*\[2\]に共著としての著者. 若者達への支えとして知られ、自尊心発達のための非営利団体 We Are Royalsを発足する。

## 略歴

アメルリスは本名テニクア・ティーヤナ・アメルリス・バレット。　カナダのオンタリオ州ニューヨーク市で生まれた。　美容師ロザリー・バレット未婚で5人の子を持つ母と12人の子を持つ父との間に生まれた。父は母を早い段階で捨て、2007年に亡くなる数カ月前に再会したのみである。母は中国系ジャマイカ人で広東省のハッカ人で"Hew"の性を持つ。父はガーナ系ジャマイカ人である。

オンタリオ州ブランプトン市の [セントエド・モンド・キャンピオン第二学校](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:St._Edmund_Campion_Secondary_School "wikilink")\[3\]へ通い、グラフィック・アーティストとして続けて[セネカ大学ニューヨーク校](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Seneca_College "wikilink") を2013年に卒業する。 ミニストリー・リーダーシップで新たに勉強を始め学位を取得する。

## モデル

[リンク=link=Special:FilePath/Nini_Amerlise_walks_for_Hendrixroe_Toronto_Fashion_week_2018_(February).jpg](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/ファイル:Nini_showcases_3_looks_for_Hendrixroe_in_Toronto_Fashion_week.jpg "wikilink") Amerlise started modelling at the age of 20, with her beginnings established on the African Fashion week Toronto\[4\] platform. In 2016, Amerlise strong walk led her to opening the show at [トロント・ファッション・ウィーク](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Toronto_Fashion_Week "wikilink") for designer House of Knot\[5\], which led to further exposure in Toronto demographic. Runway became her staple, Amerlise went on to headline shows for Canadian and International designers Hendrixroe\[6\], Candice Daniela\[7\], RVNG・Couture\[8\], Lesley・Hampton\[9\], Zoran・Dorbic\[10\], Fashion・Historic\[11\], PCP\[12\] (Greece), Rocky・Gathercole\[13\], Kenneth・ Barlis\[14\] and exclusively booked as the only female model to walk for for designer Austen Dor\[15\] at Toronto Men's fashion week.

In March 2017, She made her mark after winning Season one of Supermodel・Canada\[16\] series. Shortly after making appearances in top magazines and publications including Vogue・Italia\[17\],[Glamour](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Glamour_\(magazine\) "wikilink")\[18\], [Flare](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Flare_\(magazine\) "wikilink")\[19\], [British・Vogue](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:British_Vogue "wikilink")\[20\], [Huffington Post](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/HuffPost "wikilink")\[21\], [Daily・ Mail](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/Daily_Mail "wikilink")\[22\] UK, The cover of the [Jamaican・ Gleaner](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Gleaner_Company "wikilink")\[23\], [Brampton・Guardian](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Brampton_Guardian "wikilink")\[24\], cover of [Share Newspaper](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Share_\(newspaper\) "wikilink")\[25\], Toronto Caribbean news\[26\], [Women's wear daily](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Women's_Wear_Daily "wikilink")\[27\], Voyage LA\[28\] , [Beverly・ Hills ・Courier](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:The_Beverly_Hills_Courier "wikilink")\[29\], Glam・Africa・magazine\[30\], [Hype・Hair・magazine](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Hype_Hair "wikilink")\[31\] and Men's・ Health・ Magazine.\[32\] [リンク=link=Special:FilePath/Nini_Amerlise_Featured_model_for_Hype_Hair_Magazine.jpg](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/ファイル:Nini_featured_on_Hype_Hair_magazine_showcasing_hair_design_of_Norm_Wright.jpg "wikilink") In September 2017, she received a direct booking for [Tumbler & Tipsy](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Tumbler_and_Tipsy "wikilink")\[33\] which was released to the press for Glamour\[34\] magazine. Amerlise was later rebooked the following year in September 2018 as a featured model for the campaign which can be found in ボーグ・イタリア\[35\], and opened the show for the collection at [ニューヨーク・ファッション・ウイーク](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:New_York_Fashion_Week "wikilink"). Late September 2018, Amerlise hit stage for the Inaugural Classic New York Gala\[36\] hosted by [Mario Lopez](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Mario_Lopez "wikilink") and then made her way to Los Angeles for multiple productions including インパクト・ラジオ・ロスアンジェルス\[37\] and ironically shared the stage with Mckenzie・Olsen (Step Mother of Olsen twins) at a fashion event.

## 出演番組

[リンク=link=Special:FilePath/Nini_Amerlise_models_at_Stitched_TV_series.jpg](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/ファイル:Nini_Amerlise_models_at_Stitched_TV_series.jpg "wikilink") In April 2015, Amerlise had her first on screen appearance modelling for a designer segment on [Global TV](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/Global_Television_Network "wikilink")\[38\]. In March 2016, She appeared as one of the models for the fashion series "ゴールデン・ボタン\[39\]" aired on Afroglobal TV. On April 29th 2016, Nini was a selected model to demonstrate the product "*Smudge fix* for hit TV series Dragon's Den\[42\] aired on CBC. In October 2017, Amerlise Joined the Diversity in Fashion\[43\] panel discussion along side designer Lesley・ Hampson\[44\] and stylist Meredith Shaw for the Your Morning show on CTV. In February 2018, she shared her childhood story of resilience of how she overcame the hardships of bullying and neglect on the Caribbean・TV station GT Network\[45\], partnered with One Caribbean. In September 2018, Amerlise appeared as a model in multiple episodes for fashion television series "STITCHED". Amazingly enough, on Episode 9 Amerlise and designer "David Jack" won the series competition. \[46\], and was selected for the Marshalls commercial which showcased on Slice TV.

Amerlise signed a contract deal in South Africa on November 2019 to work as a TV Host on Travel Africa Network\[47\] and is set to film throughout the entire African diaspora in 2020 and onward.

## 慈善事業

In October 2019, Amerlise Founded the Non profit Organization "ウイー・アー・ロイヤルズ" inc , aiding in the development of confidence and positive self-esteem in youth and young adults, through the elements of arts and culture. The organization seeks out authentic stories from youth worldwide, as the Founder believes transparency produces healing. The platform has collaborated with a wide range of established artisans and industry professionals in their sphere of influence to further mentor such "Royals".

## 私生活

Amerlise, has been a devout Christian since March 2015, stating in multiple interviews that her relationship with Jesus Christ is what led to her success in the fashion and entertainment industry . Prior to the industry it was mentioned that she struggled with severe anxiety, bullying and self-image complex. Amerlise believes her faith in Jesus restored her from the inside out making her an advocate of self-love and positive esteem for the young generation.

Amerlise is very private about her dating life, however she has recently become vocal about her loving relationship with B'KEM. The two have been dating since November 2018.
