**障害詐欺**（しょうがいさぎ、）は、政府機関または民間の保険会社から不正な方法により[障害](../Page/障害.md "wikilink")に係る利益を得ることをいう。

## 障害詐欺の種類

### 一般的な種類

  - [障碍者](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/障碍者 "wikilink")に成り済して[物乞いをする行為](../Page/乞食.md "wikilink")
  - 障害者の名義を盗用
  - 障害者の身分証明書（[障害者手帳](../Page/障害者手帳.md "wikilink")など）の不正取得

### アメリカ社会保障局の基準

[アメリカの](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/アメリカ合衆国 "wikilink")[社会保障局では下記のものを障害詐欺の一つに定めている](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/社会保障局_\(アメリカ合衆国\) "wikilink")\[1\]。

  - Applicants who state they are not married when they are. Those receiving [Supplemental Security Income](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/Supplemental_Security_Income "wikilink") (SSI) can have their benefits reduced by their spouse's income and assets, so some applicants may wish to hide the existence, income, or assets of their spouse.
  - Claims of blindness. In particular, the Social Security Administration is concerned about those who declare they are blind and unable to drive, but are later found to be in possession of a [driver's license](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/driver's_license "wikilink") and are observed (legally) operating a motor vehicle.
  - Unreported income. Some types of income that often go unreported are from renting out a portion of one's home, or from an [insurance policy](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/insurance_policy "wikilink").
  - Employment changes, in a person who was not working at the time they applied for and started to receive benefits, but has since returned to work.
  - A person who is living in an institution, such as a [long-term care facility](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/long-term_care_facility "wikilink") or a [prison](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/prison "wikilink").
  - A person who is the legal custodian ([representative payee](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/representative_payee "wikilink")) of a disabled person's money spending it on some expense other than that of the disabled person.
  - A person who cashes the checks of a deceased person.
