**qBittorrent** は[クロスプラットフォーム](../Page/クロスプラットフォーム.md "wikilink")の[フリーオープンソースソフトウェアの](../Page/FLOSS.md "wikilink")[BitTorrent](../Page/BitTorrent.md "wikilink")クライアント。

qBittorrentは[C++](../Page/C++.md "wikilink")で記述されたである。[Boost](../Page/Boost_C++ライブラリ.md "wikilink")、[Qt](../Page/Qt.md "wikilink")ツールキット（バージョン 4 or 5）、-rasterbarライブラリ（torrentバックエンドのため）を使用して作られている。オプションの検索エンジンは[Python](../Page/Python.md "wikilink")で記述されている。

## 沿革

qBittorrent was originally developed in March 2006 by Christophe Dumez,\[1\] from the  (University of Technology of Belfort-Montbeliard) in France.

It is currently developed by contributors worldwide and is funded through donations,\[2\] led by Sledgehammer999 from Greece, who became project maintainer in June 2013.\[3\]

## 特徴

Some of the features present in qBittorrent include:

  - 帯域幅スケジューラ

  - Bind all traffic to a specific interface

  - Control over torrents, trackers and peers (Torrents queueing and prioritizing and Torrent content selection and prioritizing

  - Cross-platform: Windows, Linux, macOS, FreeBSD, OS/2

  - DHT, PeX, encrypted connections, LSD, UPnP, NAT-PMP port forwarding support, µTP, magnet links, private torrents

  - IP filtering: file types *eMule dat*, or *PeerGuardian*

  - Supports [IPv6](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/IPv6 "wikilink"), but cannot use [IPv4](../Page/IPv4.md "wikilink") and IPv6 at once\[4\]

  - Integrated RSS feed reader (with advanced download filters) and downloader

  - Integrated torrent search engine (Simultaneous search in many Torrent search sites and Category-specific search requests (e.g. Books, Music, Software))

  - Remote control through Secure Web User Interface

  - Sequential downloading (Download in order)

  - option

  - Torrent creation tool

  - Torrent queuing, filtering, and prioritizing

  - Unicode support, available in ≈70 languages\[5\]

## バージョン

qBittorrent is [cross-platform](../Page/クロスプラットフォーム.md "wikilink"), available on many [operating systems](../Page/オペレーティングシステム.md "wikilink"), including: [FreeBSD](../Page/FreeBSD.md "wikilink"), [Linux](../Page/Linux.md "wikilink"), [macOS](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/macOS "wikilink"), [OS/2](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/OS/2 "wikilink"), [Windows](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microsoft_Windows "wikilink").

, [SourceForge](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/SourceForge "wikilink") statistics indicate that the most popular qBittorrent version of all supported platforms, 81% of downloads, were for Windows computers.\[6\]

, FossHub statistics indicate qBittorrent as the second most downloaded software with over 75 million downloads.\[7\]

Packages for different Linux distributions are available, though most are provided through official channels via various distributions.\[8\]

## Reception

In 2012,  suggested qBittorrent as a great alternative to μTorrent, for anybody put off by recent controversial ad and [bundleware](../Page/プリインストール.md "wikilink") changes made to μTorrent.\[9\]
