**8000**（**八千**、はっせん）は、[自然数](../Page/自然数.md "wikilink")または[整数](../Page/整数.md "wikilink")において、7999の次で[8001](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/8001 "wikilink")の前の数である。

## 性質

  - 8000は[合成数](../Page/合成数.md "wikilink")であり、[約数](../Page/約数.md "wikilink")は [1](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/1 "wikilink"), [2](../Page/2.md "wikilink"), [4](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/4 "wikilink"), [5](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/5 "wikilink"), [8](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/8 "wikilink"), [10](../Page/10.md "wikilink"), [16](../Page/16.md "wikilink"), [20](../Page/20.md "wikilink"), [25](../Page/25.md "wikilink"), [32](../Page/32.md "wikilink"), [40](../Page/40.md "wikilink"), [50](../Page/50.md "wikilink"), [64](../Page/64.md "wikilink"), [80](../Page/80.md "wikilink"), [100](../Page/100.md "wikilink"), [125](../Page/125.md "wikilink"), [160](../Page/160.md "wikilink"), [200](../Page/200.md "wikilink"), [250](../Page/250.md "wikilink"), [320](../Page/320.md "wikilink"), [400](../Page/400.md "wikilink"), [500](../Page/500.md "wikilink"), [800](../Page/800.md "wikilink"), [1000](../Page/1000.md "wikilink"), [1600](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/1600 "wikilink"), [2000](../Page/2000.md "wikilink"), [4000](../Page/4000.md "wikilink"), 8000 である。
