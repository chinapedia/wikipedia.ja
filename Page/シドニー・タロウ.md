**シドニー・タロウ**（Sidney George Tarrow）は、[アメリカの](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/アメリカ合衆国 "wikilink")[政治学者](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/政治学者 "wikilink")。専門は、[比較政治学](../Page/比較政治学.md "wikilink")、社会運動論。

[シラキューズ大学](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/シラキューズ大学 "wikilink")卒業後、[コロンビア大学](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/コロンビア大学 "wikilink")で修士号、[カリフォルニア大学バークレー校](../Page/カリフォルニア大学バークレー校.md "wikilink")で博士号取得。[イェール大学](../Page/イェール大学.md "wikilink")助教授・准教授を経て、現在、[コーネル大学](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/コーネル大学 "wikilink")教授。

## 著書

### 単著

  - *Peasant Communism in Southern Italy*, (Yale University Press, 1967).
  - *Between Center and Periphery: Grassroots Politicians in Italy and France*, (Yale University Press, 1977).
  - *Democracy and Disorder: Protest and Politics in Italy, 1965-1975*, (Clarendon Press, 1989).
  - *Power in Movement: Social Movements, Collective Action and Politics*, (Cambridge University Press, 1994).（[大畑裕嗣](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/大畑裕嗣 "wikilink")監訳『社会運動の力――集合行為の比較社会学』[彩流社](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/彩流社 "wikilink"), 2006年）
  - *The New Transnational Activism*, (Cambridge University Press, 2005).

### 共著

  - *Dynamics of Contention*, with Doug McAdam and Charles Tilly, (Cambridge University Press, 2001).
  - *Contentious Politics*, with Charles Tilly, (Paradigm Publishers, 2007).

### 共編著

  - *Communism in Italy and France*, co-edited with Donald L. M. Blackmer, (Princeton University Press, 1975).
  - *Territorial Politics in Industrial Nations*, co-edited with Peter J. Katzenstein and Luigi Graziano, (Praeger, 1978).
  - *Italy in Transition: Conflict and Consensus*, co-edited with Peter Lange, (Frank Cass, 1980).
  - *From Structure to Action: Comparing Social Movement Research across Cultures*, co-edited with Bert Klandermans and Hanspeter Kriesi, (JAI Press, 1988).
  - *The Social Movement Society: Contentious Politics for a New Century*, co-edited with David S. Meyer, (Rowman & Littlefield, 1998).
  - *Contentious Europeans: Protest and Politics in an Emerging Polity*, co-edited with Doug Imig, (Rowman & Littlefield, 2001).
  - *Transnational Protest and Global Activism*, co-edited with Donatella della Porta, (Rowman & Littlefield, 2005).
