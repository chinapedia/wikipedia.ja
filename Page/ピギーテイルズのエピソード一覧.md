**ピギーテイルズのエピソード一覧**（ピギーテイルズのエピソードいちらん）は、[MTV3](../Page/MTV3.md "wikilink")で放送されているフィンランドの[テレビアニメ](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/テレビアニメ "wikilink")シリーズ『[ピギーテイルズ](../Page/ピギーテイルズ.md "wikilink")』のエピソードの一覧である。

## シリーズ概要

### 第1シーズン (2014–15)

### 第2シーズン (2015-17)

The second season focuses on pigs as they do "pigstruction" work.\[1\] A special episode based on the [Goosebumps](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/Goosebumps_\(film\) "wikilink") film was released after the series' end on 10 February 2016 in the [Angry Birds](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/Angry_Birds "wikilink")' official YouTube channel.\[2\]

### 第3シーズン (2016-17)

The third season of *Piggy Tales* is subtitled "Third Act". Consisting of a total of 35 1-minute episodes, the season's main themes are: "Summer Sports", "Back to School", "Halloween", and "Happy Holidays". All episodes are set in a theatre in downtown Pig City (as seen in *[The Angry Birds Movie](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Angry_Birds_Movie "wikilink")*), showing pigs as they "rehearse and horse around on-stage." The first episode premiered on 3 June 2016 on the iOS and Android app of [Toons.TV](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/Toons.TV "wikilink"), followed by the release on the Toons.TV [YouTube](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/YouTube "wikilink") channel on 5 June 2016. The last episode was released on 5 February 2017.\[3\] The pigs' design is based on that of their design in *The Angry Birds Movie*.\[4\]

### 第4シーズン (2018-19)

A fourth and final season, titled *4th Street*, and consisting of 30 episodes, will take place "on a street corner in Pig City where the loveable pigs put a comical spin on everyday situations."\[5\]\[6\] Despite plans to release it sometime in 2017, this season was delayed to 2018.\[7\]

Despite this delay, Rovio later released a special [Halloween](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/Halloween "wikilink")-themed sneak-peek episode of the season, titled "Scary Fog", on October 20, 2017, as part of the Angry Birds' 2017 Halloween celebration, on the *Angry Birds* [YouTube](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/YouTube "wikilink") channel.\[8\] A later one, called Holiday Heist, was released on December 16, 2017,\[9\] as the franchise celebrated [Christmas](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/Christmas "wikilink"). Around that time, however, the complete season of *Piggy Tales: 4th Street* and all of its episodes were released early during December 2017 on iTunes in *Piggy Tales Vol. 7* & *Piggy Tales Vol. 8*\[10\]\[11\] and on DVD in *Piggy Tales: The Complete 4th Season*.\[12\] These early releases on DVD & iTunes happened even before the beginning of 2018, as well as the planned schedule for all of the series' episodes to be uploaded in the *Angry Birds* YouTube channel in 2018. The official release for the series was February 10, 2018, starting with Pig City Valentine.\[13\]
