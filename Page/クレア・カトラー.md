**クレア・カトラー**（A. Claire Cutler）は、[カナダ](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/カナダ "wikilink")の政治学者。[ヴィクトリア大学政治学教授](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/ヴィクトリア大学_\(カナダ\) "wikilink")。[ブリティッシュコロンビア大学](../Page/ブリティッシュコロンビア大学.md "wikilink")大学院修了。

[国際政治と](../Page/国際政治学.md "wikilink")[国際法](../Page/国際法.md "wikilink")の交錯する領域における[権威](../Page/権威.md "wikilink")のあり方を研究。

## 著書

### 単著

  - *Private Power and Global Authority: Transnational Merchant Law in the Global Political Economy*, (Cambridge University Press, 2003).

### 共編著

  - *Canadian Foreign Policy and International Economic Regimes*, co-edited with Mark W. Zacher, (UBC Press, 1992).
  - *Private Authority and International Affairs*, co-edited with Virginia Haufler and Tony Porter, (State University of New York Press, 1999).

## 論文

  - "The 'Grotian Tradition' in International Relations", *Review of International Studies*, vol. 17, no. 1, 1991.
  - "Artifice, Ideology and Paradox: the Public/Private Distinction in International Law", *Review of International Political Economy*, vol. 4, no. 2, 1997.
  - "Public Meets Private: the International Unification and Harmonisation of Private International Trade Law", *Global Society*, vol. 13, no. 1, 1999.
  - "Locating 'Authority' in the Global Political Economy", *International Studies Quarterly*, vol. 43, no. 1, 1999.
  - "Critical Reflections on the Westphalian Assumptions of International Law and Organization: A Crisis of Legitimacy", *Review of International Studies*, vol. 27, no .2, 2001.
  - "Gramsci, Law, and the Culture of Global Capitalism", *Critical Review of International Social and Political Philosophy*, vol. 8, no. 4, 2005.
