「ロボット相撲」は、参加者が自作したロボットを力士に見立て、技術とアイデアで相手を土俵から押し出す競技の大会。世界35か国で開催されており、今では総出場台数はおよそ年間2,500台以上にものぼる。\[1\]

### 競技会

  - [RoboChallenge](http://www.robochallenge.ro/) Biggest robotics competition held in Bucharest, Romania
  - [RoboCore Winter Challenge](https://www.robocore.net/eventos?newlang=english) Biggest robotics competition held in Brazil.
  - [BattleLab Robotica](http://www.battlelab.ro/) Annual competition in Cluj-Napoca, Romania
  - [International RoboGames](http://robogames.net/) Annual competition with global sumo contestants
  - [Istrobot](http://www.robotika.sk/maine.php) Annual robotic competition in [Bratislava](../Page/ブラチスラヴァ.md "wikilink") held Mini-Sumo category until 2011
  - [Milford Autonomous Robotics Competition](http://www.robocap.org/) Annual competition near Cincinnati, OH
  - [Robofest RoboSumo Competition](http://www.robofest.net/)
  - [RobotChallenge](http://www.robotchallenge.org) Annual robotic competition in [Vienna](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/ウィーン "wikilink") with Standard-, Mini-, Micro-, Nano-, Lego- and Humanoid-Sumo
  - [Official French Tournament](http://www.robot-sumo.fr)
  - [Robot sumo Angers](http://robot.sumo.free.fr/) Annual French robotic competition in [Angers](../Page/アンジェ.md "wikilink")
  - [Southern Illinois University Edwardsville's Robot-Sumo Competition](http://roboti.cs.siue.edu/competitions/sumowrestling/)
  - [BalticRobotSumo](http://www.balticrobotsumo.org) Annual Standard-Sumo, Mini-sumo and Roomba-Sumo robotic competition in Latvia, Lithuania and Estonia
  - [Robot-SM](http://www.robotsm.se) The Swedish Robotics Championship with Standard- and mini-sumo.
