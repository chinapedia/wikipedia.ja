**アイザメ科**（Centrophoridae）は、[ツノザメ目](../Page/ツノザメ目.md "wikilink") Squaliformes の下位分類群。2属18種を含む。

## 分類

  - [アイザメ属](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/アイザメ属 "wikilink") *[Centrophorus](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/w:Centrophorus "wikilink")*
      - *Centrophorus atromarginatus* Garman, 1913 [アイザメ](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/アイザメ "wikilink")[Dwarf gulper shark](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:w:Dwarf_gulper_shark "wikilink")
      - *Centrophorus granulosus* (Bloch & Schneider, 1801) [タロウザメ](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/タロウザメ "wikilink") [Gulper shark](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:w:Gulper_shark "wikilink")
      - *Centrophorus harrissoni* McCulloch, 1915 [ハリソンアイザメ](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/ハリソンアイザメ "wikilink")[Dumb gulper shark](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:w:Dumb_gulper_shark "wikilink")
      - *Centrophorus isodon* (Chu, Meng & Liu, 1981) [Blackfin gulper shark](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:w:Blackfin_gulper_shark "wikilink")
      - *Centrophorus moluccensis* Bleeker, 1860 [オキナワヤジリザメ](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/オキナワヤジリザメ "wikilink") [Smallfin gulper shark](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:w:Smallfin_gulper_shark "wikilink")
      - *Centrophorus seychellorum* Baranes, 2003[Seycelles gulper shark](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:w:Seycelles_gulper_shark "wikilink")
      - *Centrophorus squamosus* (Bonnaterre, 1788) [モミジザメ](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/モミジザメ "wikilink") [Leafscale gulper shark](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:w:Leafscale_gulper_shark "wikilink")
      - *Centrophorus tessellatus* Garman, 1906 [ゲンロクザメ](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/ゲンロクザメ "wikilink") [Mosaic gulper shark](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:w:Mosaic_gulper_shark "wikilink")
      - *Centrophorus uyato* White, （Rafinesque,1810） [Little gulper shark](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:w:Little_gulper_shark "wikilink")
      - *Centrophorus westraliensis* White, Ebert & Compagno, 2008 [Western gulper shark](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:w:Western_gulper_shark "wikilink")
      - *Centrophorus zeehaani* White, Ebert & Compagno, 2008 [Southern dogfish](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:w:Southern_dofish "wikilink")
  - [ヘラツノザメ属](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/ヘラツノザメ属 "wikilink") *[Deania](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/w:Deania "wikilink")*
      - *Deania calcea* (Lowe, 1839) [ヘラツノザメ](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/ヘラツノザメ "wikilink") [Birdbeak dogfish](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:w:Birdbeak_dogfish "wikilink")
      - *Deania hystricosa* (Garman, 1906) [サガミザメ](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/サガミザメ "wikilink") [Rough longnose dogfish](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:w:Rough_longnose_dogfish "wikilink")
      - *Deania profundorum* (Smith & Radcliffe, 1912) [Arrowhead dogfish](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:w:Arrowhead_dogfish "wikilink")
      - *Deania quadrispinosa* (McCulloch, 1915) [Longsnout dogfish](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/:w:Longsnout_dogfish "wikilink")
