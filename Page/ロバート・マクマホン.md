**ロバート・マクマホン**（Robert J. McMahon, [1949年](../Page/1949年.md "wikilink")-）は、[アメリカ合衆国](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/アメリカ合衆国 "wikilink")の[歴史学者](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/歴史学者 "wikilink")。専門は、[冷戦](../Page/冷戦.md "wikilink")史、とくにアメリカの[東南アジア](../Page/東南アジア.md "wikilink")・[南アジア](../Page/南アジア.md "wikilink")政策。

[フロリダ大学](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/フロリダ大学 "wikilink")教授を経て、現在、[オハイオ州立大学](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/オハイオ州立大学 "wikilink")歴史学部教授。

## 著書

### 単著

  - *Colonialism and Cold War: The United States and the Struggle for Indonesian Independence, 1945-49*, (Cornell University Press, 1981).
  - *The Cold War on the Periphery: the United States, India, and Pakistan*, (Columbia University Press, 1994).
  - *The Limits of Empire: the United States and Southeast Asia since World War II*, (Columbia University Press, 1999).
  - *The Cold War: A Very Short Introduction*, (Oxford University Press, 2003).
  - *Dean Acheson and the Creation of an American World Order*, (Potomac Books, 2008).

### 編著

  - *Major Problems in the History of the Vietnam War: Documents and Essay*, (D.C. Heath, 1990).

### 共編著

  - *The Origins of the Cold War*, co-edited with Thomas G. Paterson, (Heath, 1991).

### 編纂史料

  - *Foreign Relations of the United States, 1955-1957, vol. 4: Western European Security and Integration*, co-edited with Nancy E. Johonson and Sherrill B. Wells, (United States Government Printing Office, 1986).
  - *Foreign Relations of the United States, 1955-1957, vol. 8: South Asia*, co-edited with Stanley Shaloff, (United States Government Printing Office, 1987).
  - *Foreign Relations of the United States, 1955-1957, vol. 10: Foreign Aid and Economic Defense Policy*, co-edited with William F. Sanford and Sherrill B. Wells, (United States Government Printing Office, 1989).
  - *Foreign Relations of the United States, 1955-1957, vol. 22: Southeast Asia*, co-edited with Harriet D. Schwar and Louis J. Smith, (United States Government Printing Office, 1989).
  - *Foreign Relations of the United States, 1958-1960, vol. 17: Indonesia*, (United States Government Printing Office, 1994).
