**420**（**四百二十**、よんひゃくにじゅう）は[自然数](../Page/自然数.md "wikilink")、また[整数](../Page/整数.md "wikilink")において、[419](../Page/419.md "wikilink")の次で[421](../Page/421.md "wikilink")の前の数である。

  - 420は[合成数](../Page/合成数.md "wikilink")であり、[約数](../Page/約数.md "wikilink")は [1](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/1 "wikilink"), [2](../Page/2.md "wikilink"), [3](../Page/3.md "wikilink"), [4](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/4 "wikilink"), [5](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/5 "wikilink"), [6](../Page/6.md "wikilink"), [7](../Page/7.md "wikilink"), [10](../Page/10.md "wikilink"), [12](../Page/12.md "wikilink"), [14](../Page/14.md "wikilink"), [15](../Page/15.md "wikilink"), [20](../Page/20.md "wikilink"), [21](../Page/21.md "wikilink"), [28](../Page/28.md "wikilink"), [30](../Page/30.md "wikilink"), [35](../Page/35.md "wikilink"), [42](../Page/42.md "wikilink"), [60](../Page/60.md "wikilink"), [70](../Page/70.md "wikilink"), [84](../Page/84.md "wikilink"), [105](../Page/105.md "wikilink"), [140](../Page/140.md "wikilink"), [210](../Page/210.md "wikilink"), 420 である。
