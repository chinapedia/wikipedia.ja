**棘背筋**（きょくはいきん）は、背部の[筋肉](../Page/筋肉.md "wikilink")のうち、背部深層にある筋肉の総称。棘背筋は、**固有背筋**（こゆうはいきん、musculi dorsi proprii）、**深背筋**とも呼ばれる。

## 棘背筋に属する筋

  - [長背筋](../Page/長背筋.md "wikilink")(musculi dorsi longi)
      - [板状筋](../Page/板状筋.md "wikilink")(musculus splenius)
          - [頭板状筋](../Page/頭板状筋.md "wikilink")(musculus splenius capitis)
          - [頸板状筋](../Page/頸板状筋.md "wikilink")(musculus splenius cervicis)
      - [脊柱起立筋](../Page/脊柱起立筋.md "wikilink")(musculus erector spinae)
          - [腸肋筋](../Page/腸肋筋.md "wikilink")(musculus ilicostalis)
          - [最長筋](../Page/最長筋.md "wikilink")(musculus longissimus)
          - [棘筋](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/棘筋 "wikilink")(musculus spinalis)
      - [横突棘筋](../Page/横突棘筋.md "wikilink")(musculi transversospinales)
          - [半棘筋](../Page/半棘筋.md "wikilink")(musculus semispinalis)
          - [多裂筋](../Page/多裂筋.md "wikilink")(musculus multifidus)
          - [回旋筋](../Page/回旋筋.md "wikilink")(musculi rotatores)
  - [短背筋](../Page/短背筋.md "wikilink")(musculi dorsi breves)
      - [棘間筋](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/棘間筋 "wikilink")(musculi interspinales)
      - [横突間筋](../Page/横突間筋.md "wikilink")(musculi intertransversarii)
      - [後頭下筋](../Page/後頭下筋.md "wikilink")(musculi suboccipitales)
