**クリス・ディム**（**Kris Dim**,[1973年](../Page/1973年.md "wikilink")[5月7日](../Page/5月7日.md "wikilink") - ）は[カンボジア](../Page/カンボジア.md "wikilink")出身の[ボディビルダー](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/ボディビルダー "wikilink")。

## タイトル

  - 1997 USA Championships Middleweight, 6th
  - 1998 Nationals Middleweight, 10th
  - 1998 USA Championships Middleweight, 10th
  - 1999 USA Championships Middleweight, 3rd
  - 2000 USA Championships Light Heavyweight, 4th
  - 2001 Nationals Light Heavyweight, 7th
  - 2001 USA Championships Light Heavyweight, 7th
  - 2002 Nationals Light Heavyweight, 2nd
  - 2002 USA Championships Light Heavyweight, 1st
  - 2003 Nationals Light Heavyweight, 1st
  - 2003 USA Championships Light Heavyweight, 2nd
  - 2004 Mr. Olympia 12th
  - 2004 IFBB Grand Prix England 3rd
  - 2004 IFBB Grand Prix Holland 4th
  - 2004 IFBB Grand Prix Russia 5th
  - 2004 IFBB San Francisco Pro Invitational 3rd
