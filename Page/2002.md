**2002**（**二千二**、**二〇〇二**、にせんに）は、[自然数](../Page/自然数.md "wikilink")また[整数](../Page/整数.md "wikilink")において、[2001](../Page/2001.md "wikilink")の次で[2003](../Page/2003.md "wikilink")の前の数である。

## 性質

  - 2002は[合成数](../Page/合成数.md "wikilink")であり、[約数](../Page/約数.md "wikilink")は [1](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/1 "wikilink"), [2](../Page/2.md "wikilink"), [7](../Page/7.md "wikilink"), [11](../Page/11.md "wikilink"), [13](../Page/13.md "wikilink"), [14](../Page/14.md "wikilink"), [22](../Page/22.md "wikilink"), [26](../Page/26.md "wikilink"), [77](../Page/77.md "wikilink"), [91](../Page/91.md "wikilink"), [143](../Page/143.md "wikilink"), [154](../Page/154.md "wikilink"), [182](../Page/182.md "wikilink"), [286](../Page/286.md "wikilink"), [1001](../Page/1001.md "wikilink"), 2002 である。
